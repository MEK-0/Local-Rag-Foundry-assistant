# Plant Production Line - Conveyor System Maintenance Log

## 1. Conveyor Overload Fault (Error Code: MOT-OVL-99)
- **Summary**: Occurs when mechanical resistance on the main assembly belt draws current above 15 Amps, tripping the thermal inverter.
- **Safety Warnings**:
  - Always verify that the main circuit breaker is padlocked in the OFF position before clearing blockages.
- **Step-by-step Guidance**:
  1. Inspect the driving drum pulley for tangled industrial plastic wraps or jammed physical components.
  2. Access the VFD inverter panel and verify parameter d-02 (Output Current Value).
  3. Press the blue physical Reset switch on thermal relay switch box box-04.

## 2. Belt Tracking Deviation (Error Code: BELT-ALIGN-02)
- **Summary**: Occurs when the conveyor belt drifts away from the central axis line, causing friction against the framework.
- **Step-by-step Guidance**:
  1. Locate the alignment tension bolts on the non-drive return pulley block.
  2. Rotate the left-side take-up bolt clockwise by exactly 1/4 turn increments until the drift pattern stabilizes.
